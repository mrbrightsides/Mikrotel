TENTANG KAMI

MAXONE

Maxonehotels @ Vivo Palembang is a modern hotel with a restaurant, a 5-minute walk from the Palembang Trade Center. All air-conditioned rooms have a private bathroom. Free Wi-Fi is accessible throughout the building, and on-site parking is available free of charge. The strategically located Maxonehotels @ Vivo Palembang is a 10-minute drive from Benteng Kuto Besak and Ampera Bridge, while it takes a 20-minute drive to reach Rumah Limas and Bukit Siguntang from the hotel. Sultan Mahmud Badaruddin Airport is a 15-minute drive away. The non-smoking rooms offer a flat-screen TV and a seating area, and are accessible by lift. Shower facilities are in the en suite bathroom, while towels are provided at an additional cost. For your convenience, this hotel provides an ATM machine in the area. You can pamper yourself with a relaxing massage at the spa. Hotel staff can assist with laundry services and airport pick-up. Max Bistro serves regional specialties, Indonesian food and Oriental cuisine. Food can also be enjoyed in the privacy of your room.

Located in the center of Palembang, MaxOneHotels At Vivo Palembang offers modern accommodation with an attractive unique design. MaxOne Hotels At Vivo Palembang has 113 air-conditioned rooms with private bathrooms and free Wi-Fi. Flat-screen TVs, toiletries and a seating area can also be found in each room. Enjoy Indonesian and Asian specialties at Max Bistro or with room service. ATM machines and laundry services are also available to support the comfort of your stay. At the end of a busy day, restore your fitness by enjoying massage services at the hotel spa. MaxOne Hotels At Vivo Palembang is close to the Palembang Trade Center and the famous Ampera Bridge. Sultan Mahmud Badaruddin Airport can be reached with a 15-minute drive where hotel staff can arrange pick-up services.
