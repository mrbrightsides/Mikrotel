TENTANG KAMI

THE 101 PALEMBANG RAJAWALI

Located in the fast-growing city of Palembang, South Sumatera, THE 1O1 Palembang Rajawali with its Smart and Stylish architectural Design, offers 135 chic and trendy rooms, and 2 penthouses situated on the highest floor to give the guests an eagle-eye view of the city’s landscape. Hotel facilities include four meeting rooms, a swimming pool & gym, a spa, and a restaurant serving all of the guests’ needs, both for business and leisure.

Featuring smart stylish guest rooms, a range of hotel facilities and services are available to fulfill all the guest's needs. A complimentary Wifi internet access in all areas, 24-hours in-room dining, in-room massage treatment, and free valet parking service.
