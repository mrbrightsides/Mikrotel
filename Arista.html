TENTANG KAMI

THE ARISTA HOTEL

Located in the heart of Palembang, The Arista Hotel is in a great location to explore the city! We are just steps away from the convenience of Bumi Sriwijaya LRT Station, 30 minutes away from the Sultan Mahmud Badaruddin II International Airport LRT Station, 10 minutes away from Ampera LRT Station (close to Ampera Bridge, Musi River, Kampung Kapitan and 16 Ilir Traditional Market) and 30 minutes away from Jakabaring LRT Station (close to Jakabaring Sport City), the best way to travel in palembang to avoid the heavy traffic.
