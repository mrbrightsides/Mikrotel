TENTANG KAMI

SINTESA PENINSULA

Located in the business district area of Palembang, the Sintesa Peninsula Hotel Palembang opened its door in November 2011. With only 15 minutes access to the central area and 20 minutes to Sultan Mahmud Badaruddin II International Airport, The Sintesa Peninsula Hotel is destined to become the place to meet for business.

84 magnificent Deluxe Rooms, Junior Suites, Suites and a Presidential Suite offers luxurious four star standards and with a minimum of 28 square meter of space. Sizable bathrooms with a separate shower cubicles compliment the comfortably appointed bedrooms.
